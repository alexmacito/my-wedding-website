WEDDING DETAILS

We will be celebrating our big day in Palermo, Sicily.

First, we will all meet at Villa Niscemi at 15:30 where we will say our "I DO" during our civil union.

After that, the freshly married couple will have to go to take some pictures nearby. Do not worry, while we are busy with our photoshoot, you will be making your way to the next location.

Indeed, you will be required to go from Villa Niscemi to Costa Ponente Yacht Club. There we will meet again for the reception, starting at 19:30 and will share our evening around a lovely meal and some music

TRAVEL & ACCOMODATION

If you are traveling to Palermo, the easiest way is probably taking a flight. The best option will be to land in Palermo (PMO) airport, 20 minutes away from the city center. Other alternatives are Trapani airport (TPS - 1h30 drive to Palermo) or Catania (CTA - 2h30).

We highly recommend to rent a car as it will be easier for you to move around the city and a must have if you are planning to do some tourism around this beautiful island. But if renting a car is not on your plans, do not worry! There are buses and trains from Palermo airport to the city center, just keep in mind that the wedding venues are not located in the city center. Taxis are also available if needed.

Regarding accomodations, we suggest you to look around Mondello area as it is close to both venues. You can also have a look at Sferracavallo (10min away from Villa Niscemi and 15min from Costa Ponente).

If you choose to stay in Palermo's center, calculate extra time to arrive at the wedding
